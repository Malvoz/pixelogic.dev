FAQs

  • Woh, this is cool. Where can I tune in?

    Checkout the PixelogicDev community on Twitch, Discord, or YouTube!

  • Why would you livestream programming?

    To the surprise of many people, live programming is a quickly growing community on Twitch! We believe the interest comes from the motivation and skill of other programmers on the platform. By watching other people work on their projects, you become more inspired and motivated to go out there and try it yourself!

  • Can I start my own programming livestream?

    Definitely! The more awesome people like you on the platform helps bring awareness to our community. Our goal is to get more people into programming and creating cool tools that better human's daily lives. Join our Discord to get advice on how to get started!

  • Is there a way to support PixelogicDev?

    This has always been a hobby and something that would love to be done fulltime. The best way to support PixelogicDev is through coming to the Twitch stream, subscribing to the channel, and/or checking out some awesome merch to rep the team!
